My favorite comics

1. Berserk

Guts is a mercenary. Branded as a sacrifice for God, he's forced to roam relentlessly, seeking revenge.

2. Chainsaw Man

In a world where the humans' fear take the form of devils, the Chainsaw Devil give his life to save his human friend Denji, turning him into a human-devil hybrid.

3. BLAME!

In a endlessly expanding city, Killy is searching for humans with the Net Terminal Genes, in order to connect to the Net controlling The City. This is the only way to stop the Constructors from expanding the city and disable the Safeguard, machines that kill humans without the Net Terminal Genes.
